We are an aerospace test solutions provider committed to modern, low cost, user friendly products in the quest to make space accessible to everyone.

With over 8 years of industry experience, we know how to help get things to space. Our philosophy is maintaining a small portfolio of dedicated products to provide low costs solutions that do only the things that your team needs. We are big believers in less is more.

Founded Matt La Pan has a PhD in electrical engineering and has been building dedicated software defined radio (SDR) solutions for space for a over a decade.
