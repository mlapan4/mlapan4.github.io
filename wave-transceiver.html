Software Defined

Go from transmit to receive in a few clicks. From modulating your data to digitizing and recording your RF, the Transceiver will become the swiss army knife of your RF test suite.

A powerful software based RF digitizer that records and plays back RF signals at arbitrary center frequencies and bandwidths. Reliable and easy to use. Transmit and receive telemetry for test and flight. Decommutate IRIG streams with ease. Intuitive, lightweight software that's customizable to your parameters.
  • Frequency Range: 70 MHz to 6 GHz
  • RF Bandwidth: Up to 200 MHz
  • Sample Rate: Up to 40 Msps
  • Record and playback bandwidth: Tested up to 10 Msps
  • Modem bitrate: Up to 1 Mbps
